Fenway Sports Group Banner Ads

Frozen Fenway

Frozen Fenway is an annual winter event where several college hockey teams face off against each other in Fenway Park. After contributing to the creative concepting around the campaign, "Hockey in the Wild," my next task was to design and animate a flash banner that worked alongside the print advertising to generate awareness of the event amongst college students. View the Flash Banner

Futures at Fenway

This year, the Red Sox had a lot of activities planned for their family-focused event. I worked with the creative team to generate ideas around this campaign. Our conclusion? Teasing the list of activities would drive users to the event site to learn more and purchase tickets. View the Flash Banner

Red Sox - Kids Eat Free

There's no better way to start off the baseball season than with a Fenway Frank. So when the time came to increase attendance at Fenway Park, I designed banner ads promoting free kids meals and 2-for-1 Fenway Franks. View the Flash Banner

Red Sox - January Ticket Sales (2013)

After an underwhelming season in 2012, the Red Sox tasked our team with reinvigorating their fans' spirit. But rather than using false promises and premature hype, we created a campaign that captured Boston's relentlessness and refusal to give up. View the Flash Banner
