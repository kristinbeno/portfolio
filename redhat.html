Project Background

Red Hat is the leading provider of open-source software products for the enterprise community. They came to us looking for a microsite targeted primarily to IT architects looking to extend their data centers. The purpose of this site was to generate interest around Red Hat Enterprise Linux, and would accommodate an expansive amount of content.

Flexible

In anticipation of a large amount of content in the form of videos, whitepapers and case studies, the site had to be flexible and capable of accommodating different types of content.

Prototyping

The complexity of the css animations required early prototyping of the website. I worked with the developers to build and test the prototype to ensure that it would be usable across all devices.

Retargeting Ads

To better serve their customers, Red Hat's goal was to capture information about site visitors as they engaged with the content. I designed and animated one banner ad that was later build out to many more sizes with varying messaging and imagery. The banner ads were used as a part of a retargeting campaign.
