Project Background

Founded by Peter and Carolyn Lynch in 1988, the Lynch Foundation provides assistance to programs within the Boston area. Our team was tasked with redesigning the website with three main objectives:

  • Highlight supported organizations
  • Celebrate the 25th anniversary of the foundation
  • Advance philanthropy

Style Tile

I chose to use style tiles as a place to begin my design exploration. With very little branding, other than a dated website for visual reference, it was necessary to define specific elements such as type, color palette and imagery choices prior to laying out the website. Playful colors and candid photography were used to complement the potential of the creative and youthful organizations they support. Classical elements such as the elegant background pattern and decorated border styles would serve as indications of the foundationâ€™s history and success.

Layout Design

The launch of the site coincided with the organization's 25th Anniversary event aimed at highlighting the success of the organizations they support. My team determined that featuring these success stories prominently throughout the site would encourage other promising organizations to apply.
