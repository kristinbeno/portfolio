The Lynch Foundation Digital Installation

Project Background

In addition to their website redesign project, The Lynch Foundation challenged us to design and develop a digital installation for their 25th anniversary event. This party would take place at the Artists for Humanity EpiCenter in South Boston, MA.

The Concept

It all started with the idea of an interactive timeline, showcasing the organizations The Lynch Foundation supported and the people they've impacted through their funding. After designing a mock-up of what the timeline would look like, we began developing a custom web application that would enable us to stretch, move and manipulate each piece of content displayed in the timeline.

Execution

We partnered with AVFX to project the timeline onto a 50 ft wide wall, onto 7 large physical frames constructed by Artists for Humanity and RAW Art Worksâ€”two groups funded by The Lynch Foundation. We invited guests to use the Instagram hashtag #LF25Years, which would later dynamically populate the images into the timeline frames.
