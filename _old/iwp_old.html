IWP Website

Project Background

IWP is a pharmacy service that helps people injured in accidents return to a productive life by collaborating with the legal, medical, and insurance communities. In addition to an updated website design, they came to my team looking to attract four distinct audiences; physicians, attorneys, partner organizations, and patients. With an improved user experience, IWP wanted to decrease unnecessary inbound calls from existing patients.

Style Tile

In addition to an updated online presence, IWP was looking to refresh their brand's color palette for print and digital use. Since the website look and feel would would influence the portrayal of their identity across all media, we presented several style tile options showcasing text treatments, photography, colors and interface elements.

Responsive Design

The IWP website was one of my first responsive website design projects at CTP. After completing the style tile phase of the project, the heading styles and other design elements were then mapped to wireframes my team had created. After a lot of experimentation and internal user-testing, we decided upon a mobile-friendly layout and navigation that we felt provided the best experience for IWP site users.
