About Me

While other 11-year-old girls my age were obsessing over the Spice Girls, I was designing websites. Fast-forward 15 years and not much has changed. I’m still designing websites, only now they’re carefully crafted for the people that interact with them. I believe strongly in human-centered design and enjoy the challenge of combining pleasing visual design with an experience that delights. I love sharing my passion for design with some of the best brands around, and everyday I wake up thinking… thank god I didn’t listen to those Spice Girls!

Quick Facts

You can call me Kristin, but I also go by "Beno," "KB," and "Bubba." I've been to 11 countries, my favorites being Spain, Morocco and Portugal.
