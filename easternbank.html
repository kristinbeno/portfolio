Eastern Bank Landing Page

  • View the Website

Project Background

Eastern Bank has always been committed to putting its customers and local communities first. So it only made sense for them to share their home improvement and financial tips during their spring Home Equity campaign. But they didn't stop there. They rewarded new customers with a $100 Lowe's gift card to start them off on the right foot. This landing page provided customers with more information about Eastern Bank and the tools to determine whether or not the FlexEquity account was right for them.

Making the Application Process Easy

Applying for a home equity loan or line of credit online can be daunting, so we wanted to simplify the application process for prospective customers. Once users deemed themselves ready for a FlexEquity Account with Eastern Bank, we provided them with two options for next steps. They could apply online at easternbank.com or provide contact their info so that a representative could get in touch them.
