Project Background

The goal of this project was to design a landing page that would support the “All of You” campaign for Lahey Clinic’s orthopaedic group. The site would be the home of general information about the group, such as the Lahey experience, testimonials and information about the doctors.

Design

Driven to the site through print and OOH advertising, the landing page was an opportunity to direct prospective patients to learn more about Lahey's orthopaedic department. Aimed at highlighting their holistic approach to healthcare, the website design featured a number of subjects, each with their own unique story and medical concern. The interactive content was used to encourage brand exploration, before visiting the Lahey Clinic site for more detailed information.

*This page is no longer live.
